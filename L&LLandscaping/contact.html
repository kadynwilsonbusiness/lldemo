Frequently Asked Questions

How do I get a quote?

Just call us at (414)-477-5369 or fill out the form above. We'll ask a few quick questions about your property and get back to you with a free, no-obligation estimate.

Do you offer seasonal contracts?

Yes! We offer seasonal lawn care contracts for the summer months and snow removal contracts for winter. Contracts lock in priority service and often come with a better rate than one-time calls.

How soon can you start?

In most cases, we can schedule your first service within a few days of your estimate. During peak snow season, we recommend signing up for a contract before winter starts to guarantee your spot.

What areas do you serve?

We serve Milwaukee and the surrounding communities including Wauwatosa, West Allis, Greenfield, Oak Creek, Brookfield, New Berlin, and more. Not sure if you're in our area? Just call and ask!

Are you licensed and insured?

Yes, L&L Landscaping and Snow Removal is fully licensed and insured. You can have complete confidence knowing your property and our crew are protected on every visit.
