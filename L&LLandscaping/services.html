Our Services

Complete lawn care and snow removal solutions for Milwaukee-area homes and businesses.

Lawn Mowing & Maintenance

A well-maintained lawn starts with consistent, professional mowing. Our crew arrives on schedule, cuts your grass to the ideal height for the season, and leaves your property looking sharp every time.

We handle properties of all sizes — from small residential yards to large commercial lots — with commercial-grade equipment and attention to detail.

  • Weekly & bi-weekly mowing schedules
  • Precision edging along curbs & sidewalks
  • String trimming around obstacles
  • Grass clippings blown clear of hard surfaces
  • Seasonal lawn health check-ins

Snow Plowing & Ice Removal

When Milwaukee winters hit hard, L&L is ready. We monitor weather forecasts around the clock and dispatch our plowing crews before the snow settles — so your driveway, lot, or walkways are clear when you need them.

We offer seasonal contracts and per-event pricing for both residential and commercial properties.

  • 24/7 snow response during storm events
  • Driveway & parking lot plowing
  • Sidewalk & walkway clearing
  • Pre-storm & post-storm salting
  • Seasonal contracts for guaranteed service
  • Commercial & HOA contracts available
