About L&L Landscaping

A Milwaukee-area company built on hard work, reliability, and genuine pride in every property we touch.

Milwaukee's Lawn & Snow Experts

L&L Landscaping and Snow Removal was founded right here in Milwaukee with one simple goal: give homeowners and businesses a reliable, hardworking crew they can count on in every season.

We know how demanding Wisconsin weather can be. Summers call for consistent lawn care to keep your property looking its best, and winters demand fast, dependable snow removal before ice becomes a hazard. L&L handles both — so you never have to worry about who's going to show up.

From a single-family home in Wauwatosa to a commercial parking lot in West Allis, we bring the same level of care and professionalism to every job we take on.
